Bio

I am a postdoctoral researcher at ENS Paris-Saclay under the surpervision of Jean-Michel Morel and Gabriele Facciolo. Before that, I was a PhD student at INRIA Paris, in the WILLOW project team specialized in computer vision, under the supervision of Jean Ponce and Jian Sun. I worked on hybrid methods for image deblurring, at the intersection of classical inverse problems and recent deep learning techniques. Prior to research, I received a BS and a MS from Telecom Paris and the MVA MS from ENS Paris-Saclay. During my PhD, I spent time at NYU Center for Data Science. My research focuses on computational photography and image processing, with a specific focus on image and video deblurring and super-resolution algorithms and their practical applications.
